Welcome to RABY-Ingredients

RABY-Ingredients supplying some of the world’s most prominent food, beverage, and nutrition industries with innovative functional ingredients, and quality contract manufacturing services.

  A little bit about us

RABY-Ingredients manages retail brands, cheese and ingredient manufacturing, blending and agglomeration, contract manufacturing, formulation, exports, as well as liquid dairy and RTD production. We offer the countrywide food industry a variety of products and services through three solution sectors.

We continuously invest in our world class facilities, our quality and innovation programs and most importantly our people. Customers can be confident that our rigorous quality certification guidelines, industry relationships, customer service, market insight, fluid sourcing and delivery processes are second to none.

Our team offers expertise, ingredients and services tailored to address unique production and nutritional needs. Whether the objective is to improve product texture, shelf life, nutrition, output and cost or to streamline operational processes with a turnkey contract manufacturer we have capabilities to serve.

We provide logistics support all over India, ensuring that its goods are available to as many food companies as possible. Our ingredients division offers industry leading quality, consistency, and supply of its products.

   Why Choose Us?

   Your company will obtain significant cost savings by outsourcing sample management: free up precious space in your structure to dedicate to your business.

   You will not need to have specialized personnel in handling dangerous goods and / or international shipments.

   You will have the best transportation rates thanks to our volume agreements with carriers.

   You will save on the cost of packaging whatever the type.

   You will work with a single supplier with the logistical and administrative advantages that this entails.

    You will have fewer errors and incidents by ensuring that your shipments are properly packaged, labeled and documented.

   You will have less waste by keeping an exhaustive control of your inventories.
